Transport and Tech Museums


Museum of Vinnytsia Tram

The Museum of Vinnytsia Tram introduces visitors to the history of the most popular urban transport and cars that traveled through Vinnytsia at different times. Also, the exhibits can be booked for a trip around the city.

The museum is located on the territory of a working tram and trolleybus depot, so it is a high-risk area. Therefore, you can visit it only on specially designated days and by prior arrangement.

Address: 29, Khmelnytske Hwy
Phone: +380 432 61-16-93 (reception of Municipal Enterprise Vinnytsia Trolley and Tram Administration), +380 98 004-30-57 (registration for the tour)
Website: museum.depo.vn.ua
Social media page: https://www.facebook.com/museumvinnytsiatram/


Exhibition «Vinnytsia Retro»

Retro garage with cars, motorcycles and bicycles that once drove Vinnytsia. Here you can not only look at the exhibits, but also interact with it: sit in the cabin or try to «steer». There are unique fire and sanitary vehicles, motorcycles from the Second World War, «Zaporozhets» and «Volga» of different years of production and much more from what you could previously see in the «Auto-Moto-Bike-Photo-Tele-Radio» museum.

Full-scale and reduced copies of military equipment and rarities of the American automobile industry also are presented here.

The project is implemented by the Vinnytsia Tourism Office, the Directorate of Parks and Recreation of the Territorial Community, and the NGO «Vinnytsia Museum of Transport Models, Retrotechnics, Collections and Miniatures».

Address: St. Hlibna, 1 (Central Park named after M. Leontovych, near the stadium and the «Ice Club»).

 

 

Museum of the Air Force of the Armed Forces of Ukraine

It is an open-air museum located on the territory of the headquarters of the Air Force Command of the Ukrainian Armed Forces. Therefore, it can be visited only by arranged in an advance appointment, but the experience is worth it! It features a huge collection of aircraft and air defense equipment: more than 40 units of military equipment on display and about 3,000 items of the main fund.

Address: 105, Striletska St.
Phone: +380 432 59-61-90
