Central City Park


Another popular recreational spot is the Mykola Leontovych Central City Park. It is rich in interesting objects of garden and park art. Here is one of the five stationary planetariums of Ukraine, and nearby is located the Mini-Vinnytsia Complex, which displays up to two dozen historic buildings in miniature.

And in the very heart of the park is one of the oldest and most beautiful Vinnytsia fountains, where you can see all the colors of the rainbow on a sunny day.

Park squares and alleys are successfully used by organizers of various art and gastro festivals.
