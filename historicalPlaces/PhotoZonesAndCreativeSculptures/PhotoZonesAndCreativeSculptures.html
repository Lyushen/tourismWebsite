Photo zones and creative sculptures


In recent years, many creative locations have appeared in Vinnytsia and immediately became a favorite photo area. Among the most popular — the I love Vinnytsia installation, a panel near Central Park, Liverpool Square, the Solar System fountain, and many sculptures, murals, and, of course, fountains.

A separate street photo quest will be the search for bronze works by Vinnytsia sculptor Viacheslav Posternak: saxophonist, poet, violinist, and “philosopher in the wall”.
