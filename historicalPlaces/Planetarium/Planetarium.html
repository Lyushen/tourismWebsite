Vinnytsia Planetarium


There are only 5 stationary planetariums in Ukraine, and one of them is in Vinnytsia!

Here the stars get closer, and the infinite universe reveals its secrets to us. Here you can admire the starry sky during the day and in any weather.

To touch the stars is possible thanks to the optomechanical device – Planetarium. An incredible combination of spherical film and optical projection systems creates the effect of immersion into space.

Planetarium sessions are a set of popular science and entertainment programs on astronomy and natural history that will interest both children and adults. Spherical films are also shown in foreign languages (English, Polish, etc.).

Up to 50 exciting shows, 360° full-dome projection, a 100-square-meter spherical dome screen – all so you can see more than 5000 stars with the naked eye and enjoy such amazing phenomena as: “star shower”, eclipse, aurora borealis, nebulae, sunrise, lightning, etc.

The planetarium is open every day from 10:00 to 17:00.

The program is conducted if more than 10 people are present.

On weekends and holidays, programs are offered for free visitors (no group is required) according to the event poster.

Pre-orders from organized groups are accepted for a time convenient for them.
